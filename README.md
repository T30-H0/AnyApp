Step 1: **Open Keychain Access**.


<img width="587" alt="Step1" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/e13f6428-5a46-48a8-9554-1886745ad1d4">



Step 2: Enter your email, provide your name, select **“Save to disk”**, then click **“Continue”** and finally, save it wherever you prefer.

<img width="503" alt="Step2" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/221da68d-826c-4f5d-b2a2-1ea18af23198">



Step 3: Go to https://developer.apple.com/account then in **“Certificates, IDs & Profile” select “Certificates”**


<img width="576" alt="Step3" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/7b037afb-0f99-4267-98e3-f5b14831cb26">




Step 4: Click on the **“+”** icon next to Certificates and select **IOS App Development** for ios app and then **Continue**

<img width="640" alt="Step4" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/99daff15-8541-495f-888f-69a320db5829">




Step 5: Choose the **CertificateSigningRequest** you have **created** and **Continue**, finally click **Download**.

Step 6: Open the **certificates** you just have **downloaded** and select **login** in the **Keychain** and click **Add**:


<img width="621" alt="Step6" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/fa32d131-155a-4e1f-965f-c3c00b621681">



Step 7: Back to **“Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles”** screen then select **Identifiers** and click on **“+”** to add new one, finally, select **App IDs** and **Continue**


<img width="640" alt="Step7" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/eac70e2f-ebde-4269-b5c0-49113aa69bb4">



Step 8: Select the **type** that you prefer and continue

<img width="640" alt="Step8" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/c08c6755-3150-4b42-a189-d2f23bd4ba59">



Step 9: Provide a **Description** and your **Bundle ID** for your app and click **Continue**, finally click **Continue** and then click **Register**:

<img width="640" alt="Step9" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/6601c7c5-9480-4186-8195-b94baf4192ee">



Step10: Back to the **“Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles”** screen then select **Profiles** and click on **“+”** to add a new one, finally, select **IOS App Development** or **App Store** (if you want to public your app to the app store) and **Continue**

<img width="640" alt="Step10" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/f9b24708-7aee-49b3-b5e0-445832a6f530">




Step 11: Select your app in **App Id**  and then click **Continue**:


<img width="640" alt="Step11" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/bb494aee-f2a6-4e6b-913d-defe682ddf1d">



Step 12: Select your **certificate** and then click **Continue**:


<img width="640" alt="Step12" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/c6d7d675-725d-46ef-81c6-760406c3ee07">



Step 13:  Select devices you allow to test your project and then click **Continue**:


<img width="640" alt="Step13" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/08d03794-8888-41a5-a41d-7b0a7236dbfc">



Step 14: Provide your **Provisioning Profile Name** and click **Generate**, finally click **Download**:


<img width="640" alt="Step14" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/aee0ed41-6c3e-4df3-bdac-749e5235ef01">



Step 15: **Open your Xcode => root project => Signing & Capabilities**:
In **Signing**, click on the **Team** section => select **Add an account** and enter your app account:


<img width="640" alt="Step15a" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/bea71e93-882e-4f54-b6de-c977289b68a0">

<img width="524" alt="Step15b" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/2d1d1429-c4a0-4b59-b405-f5774189a338">



Step 16: Go back to the main Xcode screen and select your team and you are done!:

<img width="640" alt="Step16" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/7c36b16f-5609-4ec2-a237-bd03b99ee066">
