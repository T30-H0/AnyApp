Step 1: **Open Keychain Access**.


<img width="550" alt="Picture1" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/fb7bd3d6-423c-4d56-98bb-549a270b4f3e">



Step 2: Enter your email, provide your name, select **“Save to disk”**, then click **“Continue”** and finally, save it wherever you prefer.

<img width="625" alt="Picture2" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/1b46a7dd-f426-48b8-9a99-2e241dd9f037">



Step 3: Go to https://developer.apple.com/account then in **“Certificates, IDs & Profile” select “Certificates”**


<img width="1440" alt="Picture3" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/b0eed9d0-1935-4cd3-82c7-aae32070a7e2">



Step 4: Click on the **“+”** icon next to Certificates and select **IOS App Development** for ios app and then **Continue**

<img width="1242" alt="Picture4" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/74ed9fdb-fd2a-4793-8e32-c3738c9a8c7c">



Step 5: Choose the **CertificateSigningRequest** you have **created** and **Continue**, finally click **Download**.

Step 6: Open the **certificates** you just have **downloaded** and select **login** in the **Keychain** and click **Add**:

<img width="547" alt="Picture6" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/7698686f-be7b-4d68-a1c3-2216a01d6d05">



Step 7: Back to **“Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles”** screen then select **Identifiers** and click on **“+”** to add new one, finally, select **App IDs** and **Continue**

<img width="1297" alt="Picture7" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/dd47a787-67b9-4591-89bc-d483b5cd70fb">



Step 8: Select the **type** that you prefer and continue
<img width="1231" alt="Picture8" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/b9bcbafc-6d82-4743-94ea-35da51768fac">


Step 9: Provide a **Description** and your **Bundle ID** for your app and click **Continue**, finally click **Continue** and then click **Register**:

<img width="1327" alt="Picture9" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/28812557-084a-4ecb-90a4-8a47ff87336a">


Step10: Back to the **“Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles”** screen then select **Profiles** and click on **“+”** to add a new one, finally, select **IOS App Development** or **App Store** (if you want to public your app to the app store) and **Continue**

<img width="1257" alt="Picture10" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/efd80394-0903-42b4-aaa4-badf6a511b1f">



Step 11: Select your app in **App Id**  and then click **Continue**:
<img width="1211" alt="Picture11" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/02a52aa0-cd6f-445a-be25-d5c911605810">



Step 12: Select your **certificate** and then click **Continue**:

<img width="1194" alt="Picture12" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/df668e2a-d627-45c2-9c4a-3fe3e3535c95">



Step 13:  Select devices you allow to test your project and then click **Continue**:
<img width="1199" alt="Picture13" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/6caeea42-8a58-47b4-b45c-cc43a2ce52ba">




Step 14: Provide your **Provisioning Profile Name** and click **Generate**, finally click **Download**:
<img width="1193" alt="Picture14" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/c921d28a-10dc-4100-84a7-0b38dcb97259">


Step 15: **Open your Xcode => root project => Signing & Capabilities**:
In **Signing**, click on the **Team** section => select **Add an account** and enter your app account:
<img width="1189" alt="Picture15" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/279a81af-74d4-4e6d-9682-096f81df67dc">

<img width="847" alt="Picture15+" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/bcb13fe5-c476-463e-a893-81f8b6e075f9">




Step 16: Go back to the main Xcode screen and select your team and you are done!:
<img width="1184" alt="Picture16" src="https://github.com/T30-H0/AnyApp/assets/107106784/59635ac0-06a9-4bdf-af67-39ebf8444119">
